Monadnock Contributors: G. Wesley Purdy

G. Wesley Purdy is a poet-philosopher who began as a nuclear submariner and electrical engineer. He presently manages a marginal existence as a sometime electrician, computer instructor/troubleshooter, food-pantry volunteer, journalist, and free-lance writer. His work in poetry, prose, and translation has appeared in numerous journals throughout the U.S., Canada, and the U.K.
