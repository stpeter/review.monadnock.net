Monadnock Contributors: William H. Barlow

The late William H. Barlow led a long and active life, working as everything from radio actor and opera singer to journalist and hospital administrator. He served with distinction as a field grade office in World War II and also acted as a French-English interpreter for Generals de Gaulle, Giraud, Juin, Eisenhower, Patton, and Clark. The poems published here were originally included in his book New Arrivals & Recent Acquisitions (Economy Press, 1988).
