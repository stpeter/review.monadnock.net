Monadnock Contributors: Stephen Marvin

Stephen Marvin is a musician who performs with Tafelmusik in Toronto and has specialized in 17th and 18th century chamber music on original instruments. He also makes bows for the violin and other stringed instruments, crafted on historical examples. Stephen has traveled to East Africa twice, and spent time with several Samburu families with whom he continues to correspond. He is currently working on a novel and a screenplay. He lives with his two Siamese cats. Website: historicalbows.com.

Communication is welcome. A printed copy of The Assumption of Nalantei is available on request. Comments, criticism, or advice may be sent to: stephenwrites@historicalbows.com.
