Peter Abelard FanSpace

Peter Abelard was a philosopher, poet-composer, lover (of Heloise), intellectual dissident, and all-around unique individual who lived during the "twelfth-century renaissance". Here at the Monadnock Review we feel Abelard is much deserving of attention for his thoughts and deeds.
