Don't send me messages, they're lies

Don't send me messages, they're lies;
don't send me messages, they're sin.
Nothing but the meeting of our eyes
in that quick glance from you when I come in.
Look at my lips. See how they burn.
My husband doesn't know to be concerned.

~

From the Italian of a Sicilian popular song (13th century).
