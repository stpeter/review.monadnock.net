Thorns

by John Enright

I hate the thorns
but love the rose
that so adorns
the bush — it grows
in its own way,
by its own laws —
and so I say
that just because
the petals' smell
so sweetly flows
in a drunken swell —
I love the rose
and the thorns as well.
