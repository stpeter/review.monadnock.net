On Monadnock

notes toward a poem...

"like a sapphire cloud against the sky"
mountain smooth and mountain strong
isolated, separate, alone
grey
circumambulate, see all sides
solitude, quiet, healing
always morning; "long morrow to this mortal youth"
new Olympus, home/haunt of giants
distant, aloof, high ideal, shining (Thayer)
American Renaissance (past and future)
"one joy it joys"
