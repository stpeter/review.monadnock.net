Transference

Big sister,
I never had:
I missed her.
It made me sad.

She'd listen
Without blinking
To my twisted
Ways of thinking —

Help me find
My own way out
Of a mind
Caught up in doubt.

Big sister
I never knew:
When I shattered,
You came through.

When it mattered
You were you.
