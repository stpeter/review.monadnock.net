Five Haiku

by Eric S. Raymond

~

Cranes rise from the lake
The autumn sun is setting
Somewhere, a flute sings.

~

A bright spring morning
The smile in a woman's eyes
These things, too, are dust.

~

Sun, gleaming on steel
First blood, exchanged between foes
This is a fierce love.

~

Washerwomen chat
River babbles over stones
Too soon, morning ends.

~

Climbing Mt. Fuji
Cherry blossoms in the wind
I see a Coke stand.

~
