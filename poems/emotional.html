"Emotional Haiku" by Peter Saint-André

~

Let frustration flow:
Watch its twisting turbulence
From the river bank.

~

Dive into your joy
Splash in its cooling waters

Anger held behind
The dam of mannered custom
Yields a greater flood.

~
