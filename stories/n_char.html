The Assumption of Nalantei

by Stephen Marvin

Glossary of Character Names

Nalantei -- young girl entering marriage, daughter of Sienti and Perean. Living in Loiyangalani, a small settlement in the Northern Frontier District of Kenya. She was born into the Lmasula clan, largest clan among the Samburu people, marrying into the Lngwesi clan, which is smaller and more traditional

Sienti -- mother of Nalantei, who has no other children. Only wife of Perean

Perean -- father of Nalantei, a respected elder among his people

"Francis" (Lesanta) -- young man, warrior (muran), and for a time, lover of Nalantei before her marriage. Educated at the mission in Loiyangalani and therefore somewhat removed from the nomadic way of life of the Samburu

Kioban -- younger friend of Nalantei. Her mother is Partuala

"Teresa" (Nirolol) -- school teacher at the mission. Young wife and close friend of Nalantei. Stays with Nalantei when her mother (Sienti) is absent

Lekapana -- age-mate and friend, "second" of Perean. He assumes prime responsibility for Nalantei's last indoctrination before her marriage

Sopoitan -- respected elder of the Lngwesi, to be married to Nalantei as his second wife. First wife is Naperu. They have a son who is a muran, and a young daughter, Maluni

Lekiso -- age-mate and friend, "second" of Sopoitan.

Naperu -- first wife of Sopoitan, mother of Maluni

Maluni -- young girl, who becomes a friend to Nalantei in her new home and clan

Nting'idua -- young married woman of Lngwesi clan. She has a newborn child

Other minor characters:

Naperu's adult son, a murani, and "Launoni" (ritual leader of the Lngwesi muran)

Partuala -- mother of Kioban

Kimiti -- murani friend of Francis

Lemakeri -- Perean's brother who lives near Mount Kulal

Sopoitan's elderly mother

"White Laibon," -- priest who directs the mission and dispensary, who in the past, preserved the lives of both Sienti and Francis
